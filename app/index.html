The Classroom:
Five Years Later
A nostalgic look back, in honor of a great friend.
Six years ago a group of friends got together to make a film. The assignment asked for a five minute documentary in Mrs. Linda Simpson's documentary film class. Instead, we made a 28 minute long mockumemtary in the style of The Office...
Trailer #1
We asked our friends to appear in the film and all of them graciously gave up their high school afternoons to be in our little production. Mr. Chris Sloan agreed to let us use his classroom and even agreed to appear in the film. Over the course of two months, we made The Classroom.
Trailer #2
At the end of it all, we arranged to show our film in Mrs. Simpson's classroom. As expectation built and word spread, we changed venues twice. Mr. Darin Hathaway agreed to let us use the auditorium to show our production and our good friend Jeremy Goldenberg agreed to run the technical aspects of the screening. On May 14th, we showed The Classroom for our classmates and friends.
Trailer #3
A mere six months later, our dear friend Michael Starks (Kevin) passed away. In our sadness, the one thing we could all take solace in was the fact that we had made The Classroom. Ever since November 21, 2008 this film has stood for something so much more than a class assignment. It is now a reminder of who Michael was and what he was willing to do for a few friends. We miss him dearly every day, and we post this film in honor of him. Enjoy.
